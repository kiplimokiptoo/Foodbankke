We welcome contributions from the community to help us improve our project. Whether it's donating food items, clothing, or volunteering your time, every contribution makes a difference in the lives of those we serve. Here are some ways you can contribute:

  1. Food Donations: We accept non-perishable food items such as canned goods, pasta, rice, and cereal. Please check our website for a list of specific items we are currently in need of.
  2. Clothing Donations:We also accept gently used clothing for all ages. Please ensure that the items are clean and in good condition.
  3. Monetary Donations: Your financial contributions help us purchase necessary supplies and support our programs. You can donate online through our website or send a check to our address.
  4. Volunteer: We are always looking for volunteers to help with sorting donations, distributing food, and assisting with events. Please contact us to learn more about volunteer opportunities.

Thank you for your generosity and support. Together, we can make a positive impact in our community and help those in need.

Contact Us: If you have any questions about how to contribute or want to learn more about our organization, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. You can contact us via email at contact@foodbank.ke.
