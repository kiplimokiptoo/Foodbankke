We welcome contributions from the community to help us improve our project. Whether it's donating food items, clothing, or volunteering your time, every contribution makes a difference in the lives of those we serve. Here are some ways you can contribute:

  1. Food Donations: We accept non-perishable food items such as canned goods, pasta, rice, and cereal. Please check our website for the latest list of needed items.
  2. Clothing Donations: We also accept gently used clothing for all ages. Please ensure that the items are clean and in good condition.
  3. Monetary Donations: Your financial contributions help us purchase necessary supplies and support our programs.
  4. Volunteer: We are always looking for volunteers to help with sorting donations, distributing food, and assisting with events.

Thank you for your generosity and support. Together, we can make a positive impact in our community and help those in need.

Contact Us: If you have any questions about how to contribute or want to learn more about our organization, please reach out via email at contact@foodbank.ke.
